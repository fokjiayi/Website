Portfolio

Applications

SMOOVote (2021)

In my second year in SMU, my team and I developed a web application which aimed to streamline the voting process for school elections.

The features of this web application are the ability to create and manage elections for your CCA, target specific voters by uploading a .csv file(e.g CCA members only), managing nominees' profiles and lastly collating election results.

I took part in devloping the interface for voters to vote during an election as well as the integration of Firebase with our web application.

ZenGarden (2021)

For my Interaction, Design and Prototyping module, my team and I came up with a prototype for an audiotherapy mediatation mobile application which targets the busy student to working adult population.

The mobile application allows users adjust their meditation sessions to fit their busy schedules. It also provides a digital private diary as well a community page for them to reflect as well as interact with other users.

Better-Q Queue System (2019)

As part of our Final Year Project at Singapore Polytechnic, my team and I worked with Watsons to create an application that helps manage incoming deliveries to the Watsons Distribution Centre.

I took up the role of one of the Front-end Developers in this project where we had to ensure that the queue system interfaces adhered to client's and users' requirements.
